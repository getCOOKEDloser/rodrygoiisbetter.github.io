The Full Story

Raised on the island of Madeira, he joined the Sporting Clube de Portugal academy at the age of eleven and signed his first professional contract in 2002. Recruited by Manchester United the following summer, he revealed his talent during Euro 2004 at only 19 years old with Portugal. He had an excellent 2007-2008 season with Manchester United, winning the Premier League and the Champions League. In 2009, he was the subject of the highest transfer in the history of football (94 million euros), when he left the Red Devils for Real Madrid. He won numerous trophies with the Madrid club, including the Spanish Championship and four times the Champions League between 2014 and 2018. Following this last success, he left Real Madrid after nine seasons at the club for Juventus Turin. His Italian adventure is punctuated by two titles of Italian champions but three successive eliminations in the Champions League. In 2021 he returned to Manchester United where he finished as the team's top scorer in his first season before being dismissed in December 2022 after publicly criticizing the club. He then signed with the Saudi club Al-Nassr FC for a record contract. In selection, he is the most capped player, the top scorer and one of the decisive players in Portugal, which won its very first international title by beating France in the final of Euro 2016 then the League of Nations in 2019 against the Netherlands. Since 2003, he has participated in five European Championships and five World Cups, of which he is the first player to have scored a goal in five different editions of the planetary competition. A complete and versatile player, he has accumulated trophies and individual records at the end of a career spanning more than twenty years. His talent and longevity make him one of the most respected players by observers despite his divisive personality. As one of the most famous athletes, he has been named the highest paid athlete in the world on several occasions by Forbes magazine, in particular thanks to his advertising contracts and commercial establishments in his name. In 2014, Time magazine included him on its list of the hundred most influential people in the world. He is also the most followed personality on the Instagram social network, with 513 million subscribers.
